About Me

My programming career began in 1997 as a Y2K Analyst and I started creating websites with HTML in 1999. Since then I have added C#, Javascript, JSON, Visual Basic and CSS to my skillset. My commercial experience lies in .NET with a focus on C#, but I also have a particular interest in developing websites that are accessible and look good on all platforms.

Additional skills include graphic design, social media marketing and librarianship.
